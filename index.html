DARE TO DREAM

District 64 Spring Conference

May 12 - 13, 2017

Calling all Toastmasters and Leaders from District 64 and beyond!

On May 12 - 13, 2017, we invite you to join us and DARE TO DREAM! That’s the theme for this year’s District 64 Spring Conference – DARE TO DREAM.
The Conference will be hosted at the Hilton Winnipeg Airport Suites. Join your friends and colleagues for an action-packed weekend of speaking, learning, having fun and, most of all – DREAMING.
We have tons of exciting events planned, including the Tall Tales and International Speech Competitions for District 64, incredible keynote speeches and banquet dinners, and interactive educational sessions.
We look forward to rolling out more details between now and then. In the meantime, we encourage you to register early for District 64’s marquee event of the year. See you in May!

Yours in Toastmasters,
The DARE TO DREAM Organizing Committee

May 12-13, 2017

Winnipeg, Manitoba
